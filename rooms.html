About Room Services

Deluxe Rooms

Relax and rejuvenate in our Deluxe Rooms. Indulge in luxury with private spa treatments, fine dining, and breathtaking views of Soyers Lake. Experience the ultimate escape from everyday life.

Our eco-friendly, organic luxury spa services are designed to help you reconnect with nature and your partner, providing an unforgettable experience.

Luxury Suites

The Luxury Suites offer the ultimate romantic getaway. These spacious rooms feature a private living area, a luxurious king-size bed, and a beautiful stone fireplace to create a cozy atmosphere. The suites offer stunning views of the lake and are equipped with an oversized Jacuzzi, perfect for two.

Room service options include gourmet meals delivered directly to your suite, an array of indulgent spa treatments, and personalized amenities to cater to your every need. Whether you’re celebrating an anniversary or enjoying a romantic escape, the Luxury Suite is a perfect choice.

Private Villas

Our Private Villas are a sanctuary of luxury and privacy, designed for couples who want to experience the highest level of indulgence. Each villa offers a private outdoor hot tub, a fireplace, and a fully equipped kitchen for a home-away-from-home experience. Enjoy breakfast on your private deck overlooking the lake or indulge in a candle-lit dinner for two in the comfort of your villa.

In-room services include bespoke spa treatments, a selection of fine wines, and personalized concierge services to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary. With unparalleled privacy and comfort, the Private Villas are the epitome of luxury.

Why Choose Our Rooms
Let's Make Your Stay Unforgettable

Soyers Lake

At Couples Resort, we think that the accommodation you select is crucial to making it a memorable stay. Our rooms are ideal for couples wishing to rekindle their romance because they are constructed with comfort, intimacy, and luxury in mind. Every room offers stunning views of Soyers Lake and is furnished with amenities to meet all of your needs, whether you want the private and exclusive setting of a Private Villa, the roomy elegance of a Luxury Suite, or the comfortable atmosphere of a Deluxe Room. Every element, from private Jacuzzis to in-room fireplaces, has been thoughtfully chosen to create the ideal romantic retreat.

The LeBlanc Family

Our rooms are an experience rather than merely a place to stay. Imagine having a private breakfast on your balcony, enjoying a couples' spa treatment in the luxury of your own home, or waking up to a tranquil view of the lake. Our rooms provide the ideal haven whether you're looking for romance, adventure, or relaxation. Selecting a stay at Couples Resort entails selecting a restorative and intensely private experience intended to produce enduring memories, complete with individualized attention, opulent facilities, and the peace of nature everywhere.
